How We Work Together

Engagement models built around
the way decisions actually get made

Every engagement is structured to produce a clear deliverable and a clear next step. No open-ended retainers that stall. No reports that sit on shelves.

Sprint

Diagnostic Sprint

2–3 weeks

A rapid assessment of a specific challenge — strategy gap, capability shortfall, or vendor decision — that produces a clear point of view and an actionable path forward. Designed for leadership teams that need an answer, not a process.

01Intake & scoping — Align on the question, the decision-makers, and what a useful output looks like
02Rapid discovery — Structured interviews, document review, and benchmark comparison against peer firms
03Synthesis & readout — A written findings memo and an executive readout with a prioritized set of recommendations

Sprint

AI Use Case Prioritization

1 day or 4 weeks

Structured prioritization of AI opportunities across your firm — scored against business value, feasibility, and strategic fit. Available as a focused workshop or a deeper four-week engagement.

01Use case identification — Collaborative ideation session across business lines to surface the full landscape of AI opportunities
02Scoring & prioritization — Evaluate each use case against a defined rubric: value potential, implementation complexity, data readiness, and organizational fit
03Business case development — Build out the top-priority use cases with problem statements, solution concepts, and a high-level ROI framework
04Roadmap & executive presentation — A sequenced implementation roadmap and board-ready materials for the 4-week engagement

Project

Vendor Selection

6–10 weeks

End-to-end support for high-stakes platform decisions — from requirements through contract. Structured to reduce evaluation bias, surface the real trade-offs, and give leadership a defensible recommendation.

01Requirements definition — Stakeholder workshops to define functional, technical, and operational requirements and scoring weights
02Market scan & long-list — Independent landscape review; down-select to a qualified short-list with rationale
03RFP & demos — Structured RFP, facilitated demos, and reference checks against your requirements, not vendor talking points
04Decision support & negotiation — Scoring summary, final recommendation memo, and negotiation guidance through contract close

Program

Program Leadership

3–12 months

Embedded senior oversight for large-scale implementations, platform deployments, and operating model transformations. Designed for programs where the stakes are high enough to warrant a dedicated senior lead.

01Program structure — Define workstreams, governance model, RACI, and a realistic delivery roadmap
02Ongoing delivery management — Weekly cadence, issue escalation, stakeholder alignment, and executive reporting throughout
03Milestone reviews — Structured stage-gate reviews to catch scope drift, surface blockers early, and keep the program on trajectory
04Close & transition — Documented outcomes, lessons learned, and a clean handoff to the internal team

Ongoing

Advisory Retainer

Ongoing · Monthly

A standing relationship for executives who need a senior, independent sounding board — on strategy, technology decisions, board materials, or emerging issues. Structured as a monthly commitment with defined access.

01Onboarding — Structured intake to understand your firm's strategy, priorities, and key decisions on the horizon
02Monthly engagement — Scheduled calls, written briefs, and rapid-response access for time-sensitive questions
03Deliverables as needed — Point-of-view memos, presentation reviews, and vendor assessments on an as-needed basis within the retainer scope
